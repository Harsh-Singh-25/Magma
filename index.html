What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think and act like humans. It allows computers and systems to perform tasks such as problem-solving, learning from experience, understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions with minimal human intervention.

AI can be categorized into two types: narrow AI (which is designed for a specific task, like voice assistants) and general AI (which would perform any intellectual task a human can do).

It powers technologies like chatbots, recommendation engines, self-driving cars, and facial recognition. Machine Learning (ML), a subset of AI, enables systems to learn and improve over time without being explicitly programmed. Deep Learning, a more advanced form of ML, uses neural networks to process vast amounts of data.

AI continues to revolutionize industries like healthcare, finance, education, and entertainment, making our lives more efficient and connected.

What is
Magma?

Web3 platform

Magma is a Web3 platform for all real estate stakeholders, which provides users with the ability to manage interactions between physical and digital assets through unique, and collectively generated, 3D representations of their buildings.

The Digital Twin Token: The building’s unique digital copy

Magma uses blockchain technology to record essential data, which enables transparent, traceable, and immutable streams of information to be readily accessible to its users. Magma smart contracts are built with this blockchain data to expedite tasks like requests for proposals, lease agreements, and services arrangements.

An Index of digitization

The Magma Index provides valuable insight for decision-making regarding data-sharing, service-creating, and operational/environmental expectations.
